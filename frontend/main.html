To-Do List

Stay organized with a clean, intuitive to-do list that helps you focus on what matters. Create tasks, set priorities, and check items off as you complete them. The layout is designed to reduce clutter and make progress feel effortless.

Progress Bar

Track your productivity at a glance with a dynamic progress bar that updates as tasks are completed. This feature gives immediate visual feedback, helping you stay motivated and aware of how close you are to reaching your goals.

Branches

Group related tasks into branches to keep projects clearly separated. Whether you’re managing work, personal goals, or long-term plans, branches help you stay structured and avoid overwhelm.

Task Notes

Attach notes to tasks so important details are never lost. Whether it’s links, reminders, or quick thoughts, task notes keep everything in one place.

Consistency Tracker

Track daily completion streaks to reinforce consistency and build better habits. Seeing your streak grow adds a subtle but powerful motivation boost.
